Manhattan Meatballs

Description

Per Serving: 300 calories; protein 14.5g; carbohydrates 43.2g; fat 7.6g; cholesterol 65.1mg; sodium 598.6mg.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds extra lean ground beef
  • 2 cups dry bread crumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 ½ (16 ounce) jars pineapple preserves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium-size mixing bowl, mix barbecue sauce and pineapple preserves together.
  3. In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine meat, breadcrumbs, eggs, onions, salt, and parsley.
  4. Mix well and form into bite-size balls.
  5. Arrange the balls in a single layer in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Pour the barbecue sauce mixture evenly over the meatballs.
  7. Bake for 30 to 45 minutes, until the meat is cooked
