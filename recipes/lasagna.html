Cheesy Sausage Lasagna Soup

Description

Per Serving: 281 calories; protein 24.6g; carbohydrates 19.8g; fat 12.3g; cholesterol 75.4mg; sodium 2113.4mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian turkey sausage, casings removed
  • 2 cups chopped onions
  • 2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-seasoned diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup uncooked mafalda pasta
  • 2 cups chopped fresh spinach
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Â¼ cup Parmesan cheese
  • 4 teaspoons thinly sliced fresh basil

Steps

  1. Cook and stir sausage in a large pot until browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in onions, mushrooms, and garlic.
  3. Pour in chicken broth, tomato sauce, and diced tomatoes; bring soup to a boil.
  4. Stir pasta into the soup.
  5. Cook, stirring occasionally, until almost tender, about 10 minutes.
  6. Stir in spinach, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Remove from heat; stir in basil and let stand until pasta is tender, about 2 minutes more.
