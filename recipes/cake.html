Sad Cake

Description

Per Serving: 218 calories; protein 2.3g; carbohydrates 28.7g; fat 11.1g; cholesterol 31mg; sodium 152.2mg.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups biscuit baking mix
  • 2 ¼ cups packed brown sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup flaked coconut
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Grease and flour one 9x13 inch pan.
  3. Mix thoroughly together the biscuit mix, brown sugar, eggs, flaked coconut, chopped pecans, and vanilla.
  4. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 to 40 minutes.
  6. Allow cake to cool before cutting.
